Biography

Ratan Naval Tata is an Indian industrialist, philanthropist, and former chairman of Tata Group, one of India's largest conglomerates. He served as the chairman from 1991 to 2012, and again, as interim chairman from October 2016 to February 2017. During his tenure, Tata transformed the company into a global business, with acquisitions like Jaguar Land Rover, Corus Steel, and Tetley Tea. He is widely admired for his humility, vision, and dedication to ethical business practices. Ratan Tata is also a recipient of India's two highest civilian awards: the Padma Bhushan and Padma Vibhushan.
